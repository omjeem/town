## Notes

- **Library exteriors** reuse generic-building + school sprites; the interior (museum-style gallery rooms) is what makes it a library.
- **Museum exteriors** also reuse generic-building + school; museums share interior set with Library.
- **Station exteriors** - `20_Subway_and_Train_Station` ships only train cars, no station buildings; the cars are placeholders. Consider mixing with a generic-building or school sprite for the actual station hall.
- **Cafe exteriors** - no dedicated theme; reuses small market-stall sprites from Shopping_Center_and_Markets.
- **Office exteriors** - `16_Office_Singles_16x16` is almost entirely modular wall/balcony tiles. Only the two `Example_*` sprites are whole-building. Hospital/Hotel singles + Generic_Building condos are useful fallbacks.
- **Interiors** are `Layer_1` (renderable base) plus `preview` (flat composite). `Layer_2`/`Layer_3` overlays are skipped - they'd need separate stacking logic.
- **Office interiors** are `.gif` (animated). They're the only format shipped in `Modern_Office_Revamped_v1.2/6_Office_Designs/`; the source `.aseprite` files would need export.
- **Condo interiors** are listed both under Home (via Condominium_Designs) and as a bonus standalone plot in case you split residential into Home vs Condo.
